ST.ALOYSIUS PRE UNIVERSITY COLLEGE

St. Aloysius Pre-University College (PUC), located in Mangalore, Karnataka, is a well-established institution with a long history of academic excellence. Its structure, teaching methods, and emphasis on sports make it a prominent educational institution in the region.

1.Structure:

St. Aloysius PUC operates under the St. Aloysius Institutions, which are managed by the Jesuits, a religious order known for its dedication to education. The campus boasts modern facilities, including well-equipped laboratories for science students, computer labs, and libraries. The classrooms are designed to provide a conducive learning environment, and the campus also has specialized rooms for different streams such as science, commerce, and arts.

3.Sports:

St. Aloysius PUC places a strong emphasis on sports and physical education, providing students with the opportunity to excel in a variety of sports. The institution has well-maintained sports grounds and facilities for basketball, volleyball, handball, football, cricket, and athletics. The college regularly participates in inter-college and state-level competitions, promoting a culture of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. There is a dedicated sports department that organizes events and ensures students get the necessary training and guidance.
